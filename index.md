I am **Yuantao Wei**, an M.S. student in Industrial Engineering at the University of Washington (expected June 2026).

My work focuses on building practical, data-driven methods for complex decision systems at the intersection of:
- Digital Twin
- Machine learning
- Multi-agent simulation
- AI-assisted scientific modeling

I am currently working on:
- **Urban emergency evacuation simulation** using large-scale multi-agent models and spatial data.
- **LLM-enhanced Bayesian network structure learning** to reduce expert intervention cost.
- **Causal inference and LLM uncertainty modeling** with human-in-the-loop reasoning patterns.

Before UW, I worked across finance, consulting, and medical-device settings on end-to-end algorithm workflows, from data engineering to model validation and deployment.
