## Research Projects

### Multi-Agent Urban Emergency Evacuation Simulation
**Role:** Research Assistant  
**Period:** 2025 - Present  
**Focus:** Large-scale evacuation modeling in urban networks under dynamic policy constraints.
- Built a multi-agent simulation framework using OpenStreetMap-derived spatial data.
- Developed a statistical analysis pipeline (correlation, mutual information, random-forest regression) to identify key drivers of evacuation outcomes.
- Investigated threshold effects and behavioral phase shifts for decision support in shelter placement and guidance strategies.

### LLM-Enhanced Bayesian Network Structure Learning
**Role:** Research Assistant  
**Period:** 2024 - Present  
**Focus:** Improving Bayesian optimization efficiency with local LLM agents and structured expert knowledge.
- Reproduced and deployed the LLAMBO framework with local LLM inference through Ollama.
- Incorporated expert-knowledge elicitation modules to reduce intervention cost and improve search efficiency.

### Causal Inference and LLM Uncertainty
**Role:** Research Assistant  
**Period:** 2024 - Present  
**Focus:** Modeling human causal reasoning patterns and uncertainty behavior in LLM-assisted systems.
- Designed expert questionnaires for causal reasoning data collection.
- Built regression-based models to approximate intervention cost and uncertainty patterns.
