## Courses Taught
[0] **Statistical Quality Control** — Teaching Assistant

This course provides the theoretical foundation needed to design, implement, and interpret statistical quality control tools (such as control charts) across diverse applications.

Students develop a rigorous scientific basis for using statistics in quality control and for designing and interpreting control charts.

The course also includes a reading project in which students read and present a research article on real-world applications of statistical quality control methods written by experienced professionals and researchers.
